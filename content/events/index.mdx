# Events

Events drive the execution of workflows and actions in your AI applications, enabling real-time responsiveness to business conditions.

## Overview

The Events system provides a way to detect, process, and respond to events in your business applications. Events include:

- **Triggers**: Initiate workflows based on detected events or conditions
- **Searches**: Query and retrieve data for decision-making within workflows
- **Actions**: Perform tasks and operations within workflows

## Business Value

Event-driven architecture enables your business applications to:

- Respond to changes in real-time
- Automate complex business processes
- Integrate with external systems
- Scale operations without proportional complexity
